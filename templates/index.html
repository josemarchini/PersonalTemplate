About me

Hi, i'm Jose. I have created this website with some of the technologies I use (Python-Flask, MySQL, JS, HTML & CSS) with the intention that you know me a little more, I am passionate about technology and numbers from a very small age and now I am turning this passion into a job.
I really like working with data and making an application work from the bases, that’s why I decided to specialize in the back-end part and in innovative technologies such as Blockchain that in the end are a type of distributed data base with a peer-to-peer architecture, which means that the information is kept distributed on all nodes of the network and not on a central node.

On this website you will probably find some examples of my work, do not forget to try the functionalities and come back periodically because I am in expensive learning and very likely you will find improvements and new things over time ;).
